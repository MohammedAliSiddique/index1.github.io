About Us

"Dreamweaver" is a social enterprise which strives to empower artists and artisans. Dreamweaver aims to connect the unconnected artisans to the local and international markets by not only providing them with a market place but also marketing them in an effective way. Dreamweaver works for the revival of the dying crafts by fusing them with modern and marketable designs and by giving design consultation to the artisans. We, at Dreamweaver, consider artisans as one big family and reach out to them wherever they need us.
